📖 BODMAS Reference Card

Always apply these rules in order — higher tiers must be solved first!

B
Brackets — ( ) Solve everything inside brackets first, regardless of what operators are inside. 2 × (3 + 4) = 2 × 7 = 14
O
Orders — Powers & Roots After brackets, evaluate any exponents (powers/squares/cubes). 2 + 3² = 2 + 9 = 11
D
Division ÷ Division and Multiplication have equal priority. Solve left to right. 12 ÷ 3 × 2 → first 12÷3=4, then 4×2=8
M
Multiplication × Same priority as division. When both appear, work left to right. 4 + 3 × 2 → first 3×2=6, then 4+6=10
A
Addition + Addition and Subtraction have equal priority. Solve left to right. 10 − 3 + 2 → first 10−3=7, then 7+2=9
S
Subtraction − Last priority (tied with Addition). Always apply left to right. 8 + 2 × 5 → first 2×5=10, then 8+10=18
💡 Memory tip: "Brackets Over Division & Multiplication, Addition & Subtraction"
